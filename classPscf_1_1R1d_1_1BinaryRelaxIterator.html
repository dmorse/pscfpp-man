Detailed Description

Relaxation iterator for SCF equations for two-monomer system.

This class implements the simple Picard-type relaxation iterator for systems with two monomer types that was introduced by Drolet and Fredrickson (PRL, 1999).

Reference: F. Drolet & G.H. Fredrickson, Phys. Rev. Lett. vol. 83, 4317 (1999).
