Detailed Description

template<int D>
class Pscf::Pspg::Domain< D >

Spatial domain and spatial discretization for a periodic structure.

A Domain has (among other components):

  • a Mesh
  • a UnitCell
  • a SpaceGroup
  • a Basis
  • an Fft
  • a WaveList
  • a FieldIo
  • a lattice system enumeration value
  • a groupName string
