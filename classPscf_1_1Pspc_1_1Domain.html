Detailed Description

template<int D>
class Pscf::Pspc::Domain< D >

Spatial domain and spatial discretization for a periodic structure.

A Domain has (among other components):

  • a Mesh
  • a UnitCell
  • a SpaceGroup
  • a Basis
  • an FFT
  • a FieldIo
  • a lattice system enum value
  • a groupName string
